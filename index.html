Stocks Scanner

The Problem: How do we know which companies are worthy of our attention and investment in the long run?

In this Project we are using Web Scraping with Proxies inorder to
recieve relevent Sec Filling Information for the evaluation of the company.

  1. First Step: Get list of Proxies to use for web scraping so the website couldn't block our visits

  2. Second Step: Get The relevant data from the website and perform the neccesary calculations and formation

  3. Third Step: Export to Excel three sheets
    (Company evaluation throught the years ,company rate values computation and P/E Ratios)

  4. Fourth Step: Conduct condition based on rate calculations that will select and export worthy companies tickers to text file

  5. Final Step: Conduct modification on the company evaluation sheet and the calculations sheet
    • In company evaluation sheet the rules are: the negative % numbers will be RED
      and positive % numbers will be GREEN
    • In company calculations sheet the rules are: % numbers < 10 will be RED
      and % numbers > 10 will be GREEN
